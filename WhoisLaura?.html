Laura Greene

Solving Problems since 1975

Laura Greene has been an opera singer, piano teacher, firefighter, naturalist, tour guide, fisheries biologist, photogrpaher, writer, and international volunteer for conervation organisations including The Nature Conservancy, and Cheetah Conservation Society. She is now turning her sights to learning coding and webdesign to bring her creative vision and passion for conservation to the software development world. She hopes to create tools, apps, and compelling interactive websites for conservation orginisations to faciliate engagement with a public who is boecoming increasingly detached from the natural world. She believes technology can serva as a bridge between the world outside and the world within.
